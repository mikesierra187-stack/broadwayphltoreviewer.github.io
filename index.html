License Categories

Student Permit (SP)

Description: A beginner's permit allowing supervised driving only. You must be accompanied by a licensed driver (21+ years old with at least 1 year of licensing experience).
Validity: 1 year.
Minimum Age: 16 years.
Vehicle Categories: Limited to light vehicles (e.g., cars under DL Code B).
Purpose: Prerequisite for applying for a full license; requires passing a theoretical driving course.
Key Note: Not a full license‚Äîcannot drive alone.

Non-Professional Driver's License (NDL)

Description: For personal, non-commercial use. Allows independent driving of specified vehicles.
Validity: 5 years (10 years if no violations in the last 5 years).
Minimum Age: 17 years (after holding SP for 1 month).
Vehicle Categories (DL Codes):
A, A1,B,B1,B2
Key Note: Cannot be used for paid driving (e.g., taxi, delivery). Requires passing written and practical exams.

Professional Driver's License (PDL)

Description: For commercial/professional driving (e.g., public transport, trucking).
Validity: 5 years (3 years if the holder is 61‚Äì65 years old; not issued after 65 unless for private use).
Minimum Age: 18 years (requires holding NDL for at least 6 months).
Vehicle Categories (DL Codes): Includes all NDL categories plus heavier vehicles {C,D,BE,CE}.
Key Note: Requires additional training (e.g., PDC from accredited schools) and is mandatory for for-hire drivers. Higher insurance and safety standards apply.
